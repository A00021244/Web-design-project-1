About John O’Reilly

Hey there, I'm John O’Reilly, the proud owner of Wheels on Fire. I've been in the car business for as long as I can remember, and I love a well-tuned engine. I’m a bit of a perfectionist — maybe even more with cars than with people, if I’m honest. This site showcases my collection and helps you find your perfect ride, all with a touch of my dry humour.
